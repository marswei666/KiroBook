Kiro Book is a digital travel journal for people who love exploring the world.

The app is designed for travelers who want to remember the places they visit across different countries and cities. It helps users log beautiful cafes, bookstores, museums, galleries, exhibitions, select shops, bars, restaurants, and other memorable spots. Instead of leaving these moments scattered across a photo library, Kiro Book provides a focused personal journal for recording and organizing them.

## Privacy Policy

Kiro Book takes your privacy seriously.

Data Collection: This app does not collect, store, or upload any personal photos. It only tracks the number of check-ins.

Local Storage: All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Third-party Sharing: No data is shared with third parties.
